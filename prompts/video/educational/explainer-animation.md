# Explainer Animation

## Prompt
Educational explainer animation, clean modern style, simple shapes, smooth animations, professional motion graphics, clear typography, infographic style, 2D animation, colorful but professional, smooth transitions, 4K, 60fps, educational content, easy to understand, minimalist design, professional presentation

## Image
![Explainer Animation](./explainer-animation.png)

## Creative Thinking

This prompt is designed for educational video content. The emphasis on "clean modern style" and "simple shapes" ensures clarity, which is essential for learning materials. "Smooth animations" and "smooth transitions" maintain viewer engagement without being distracting. The combination of "colorful but professional" balances visual appeal with credibility. "Infographic style" and "2D animation" guide the AI toward clear, digestible visuals rather than complex 3D renders that might overwhelm educational content.
