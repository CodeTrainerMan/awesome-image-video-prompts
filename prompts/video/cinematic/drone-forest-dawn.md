# Drone Forest Dawn

## Prompt
A cinematic drone shot slowly ascending over a misty forest at dawn, revealing a hidden lake, smooth camera movement, golden hour lighting, dramatic clouds, 4K resolution, cinematic color grading, professional cinematography, smooth motion, 60fps, film grain, anamorphic lens flare, epic scale, nature documentary style

## Image
![Drone Forest Dawn](./drone-forest-dawn.png)

## Creative Thinking

This prompt is designed to create a cinematic nature scene with dynamic camera movement. The key elements are the slow ascending drone movement which creates a sense of discovery and scale. The "misty forest at dawn" establishes atmosphere, while "revealing a hidden lake" provides a narrative payoff. Adding "60fps" and "smooth camera movement" ensures fluid motion, crucial for video generation. The combination of "cinematic color grading" and "anamorphic lens flare" adds professional film aesthetics. The "nature documentary style" helps guide the AI toward realistic, immersive visuals rather than overly stylized effects.
