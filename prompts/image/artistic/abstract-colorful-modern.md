## Creative Thinking

The design philosophy of this prompt is balancing abstract and concrete elements. By using both "fluid shapes" and "geometric patterns", it creates works that have both organic and structural qualities. Explicitly listing colors (blue, purple, pink, orange, yellow) ensures the generated artwork is rich and harmonious in color. "High contrast" and "dynamic composition" enhance visual impact, while "contemporary art" guides the AI to generate works that align with modern aesthetics.
